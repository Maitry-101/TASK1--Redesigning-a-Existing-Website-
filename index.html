About BIT DURG

Bhilai Institute of Technology (BIT), Durg, established in 1986, is a premier engineering institution in Chhattisgarh, India. Accredited by the NBA and NAAC with an 'A' grade, BIT Durg is committed to academic excellence, research innovation, and fostering a vibrant student community.
